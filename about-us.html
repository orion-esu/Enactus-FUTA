Our Mission

Engage the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders In The use Of innovation and business principles to improve the world.

Enactus is an international nonprofit organization dedicated to inspiring students to improve the world through entrepreneurial action.
We as a branch in Federal University of Technology, Akure Nigeria, create community development projects that put people's own ingenuity and talents at the center of improving their trajectory of their lives. Guided by educators and supported by business leaders, we students take the kind of entrepreneurial approach that empowers people to be a part of our own success. Our work transforms both the lives of people we serve, and in turn, the lives of we students as a collective team as we develop into highly effective, values-driven leaders.

Our Values

We live our values of integrity, passion, innovation and collaboration in all that we do.

Entrepreneurial – igniting business innovation with integrity and passion.

Action – the experience of social impact that sparks social enterprise.

Us – student, academic and business leaders collaborating to create a better world.
