About Us

Enactus is an international nonprofit organization dedicated to inspiring student to improve the world through entrepreneurial action. We as a branch in Federal University of Technology, Akure, Nigeria, create community development projects that put people's own ingenuity and talents at the center of improving the trajectory of their lives.
