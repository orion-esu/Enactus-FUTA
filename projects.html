T.R.E.E an acronym coined from Transform, Renovate, Educate and Empower, was a project geared towards renovating St. Andrewa Anglican Primary School, located in Arakale Community, Isinkan. Enactus FUTA realized that it was much more than renovating the school so the team decided to adopt the school.

Project Bubbue

The project provided an avenue for the empowerment of individuals in the community willing to offer affordable laundry services but lacks the capital often associated with setting up standard laundry business outlets. While this would serve as an alternative source of income for many, it will also usher in accessible and affordable laundry service for the community members.
